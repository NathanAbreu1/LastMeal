What is LastMeal?

LastMeal is a restaurant literally at the end of the universe, where you will experience the best food you could ever have in your entire life. as part of the show, you will see the entire universe passing through your eyes in a spectacular show created by LM production, meanwhile, you will be eating your meal with one of the most delightful experiences that the universe could've ever provided you. Something very important to notice is that whenever you try this food, the answer to life, the universe and everything is part of one of the main ingredients of the food, leading to a very plesurable and unforgetful experience.
